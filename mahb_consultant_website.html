Our Services

A full suite of tax, legal, and business services for individuals and companies across Pakistan.

NTN Registration

We assist individuals and businesses in obtaining their National Tax Number (NTN) swiftly and correctly, ensuring compliance with FBR requirements.

Income Tax

Expert advisory and filing services for income tax returns. We handle salary, business, and rental income declarations for salaried and self-employed clients.

Sales Tax

Sales tax registration, return filing, and reconciliation services. We ensure your business stays compliant with FBR's sales tax regulations at all times.

Tax Filing

Timely and accurate filing of annual and quarterly tax returns for individuals, sole proprietors, partnerships, and corporate entities.

Accounting & Bookkeeping

Comprehensive bookkeeping, ledger maintenance, financial statement preparation, and management accounts for small to large businesses.

Company Registration & Incorporation

Complete SECP registration, company incorporation, and annual filing services for private limited companies, SMCs, and LLPs.

IPO — Trademark, Copyright, Patent & Design

Protect your brand and innovations through IPO Pakistan. We handle trademark filing, copyright registration, patents, and industrial design applications.

Chamber of Commerce Registration

Assistance with membership and registration at local and regional Chambers of Commerce to enhance your business credibility and networking.

EOBI & PESSI Registration

Employee registration, contribution management, and compliance with EOBI (Employees Old-Age Benefits Institution) and PESSI social security requirements.

Custom Clearing

Professional customs clearance services for imports and exports, documentation handling, duty assessment, and liaising with customs authorities.

Import / Export & Shipping

End-to-end trade facilitation including import/export licensing, freight forwarding, shipping coordination, and regulatory compliance.

Business Advisory

Strategic business advisory services covering tax planning, financial structuring, and regulatory guidance to support your business growth.

Who We Are
Your Reliable Tax & Business Partner

Mahb Consultant is a professional tax consultancy and business services firm serving clients across Pakistan. With over a decade of experience, we have built a reputation for accuracy, reliability, and personalized service.

Our team of qualified consultants and legal experts specializes in tax compliance, company registration, intellectual property, trade facilitation, and social security services — ensuring every client receives expert guidance tailored to their specific needs.

We operate from two strategically located offices in Multan and Chakwal, providing convenient access to clients across Punjab and beyond.
